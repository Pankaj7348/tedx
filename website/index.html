What is Ted?

TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is
a globally recognized nonprofit organization dedicated
to spreading impactful ideas through concise
and thought-provoking talks.
Since its inception in 1984, TED has evolved
into a platform that showcases expert insights on science,
technology, business, innovation, psychology,
and global issues. TED Talks are highly curated,
no longer than 18 minutes, and are delivered
by world-renowned thought leaders,
researchers, and innovators.
